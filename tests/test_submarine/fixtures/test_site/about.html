About Grace Community Church

Grace Community Church has been serving the Springfield community since 1987. We are a non-denominational church committed to helping our neighbors in need.

Our Food Pantry Ministry

Started in 2003, our food pantry has grown from serving 20 families per month to over 200. We partner with the Central Illinois Food Bank and local grocery stores to provide fresh, nutritious food to families in need.

Our dedicated team of 15 volunteers sorts and distributes food three days a week. We also offer nutrition education and cooking classes quarterly.

Other Ministries

  • After-school tutoring program
  • Senior companion visits
  • Annual back-to-school supply drive
